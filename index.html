THE ORIGIN

Casablanca Youth

Witnessed street violence that shaped his intense lyrical style.

Musical Roots

Exposed to Bob Marley and Otis Redding by his father (a merchant sailor), while his mother introduced him to Cher.

Street Culture

Originally envisioned himself as a street dancer. He was part of a Krump group until 2011.

Pre-Fame

Before committing to music in 2016, he worked as a call center agent, grinding to make his dream a reality.
